Chapters

Chapter 1: The Good Old Days

Bobby spent most of his time with his katropa during Grade 8. Life was cheerful, friendships were strong, and he felt a sense of belonging that he would soon lose.

Chapter 2: The Separation

During the enrollment for Grade 9, Bobby was placed in Section 9-Golf while his katropa were assigned to 9-India. The sudden separation left him feeling isolated and frustrated.

Chapter 3: Initial Struggles

The school year 2017-2018 began, and Bobby struggled to adjust to his new section. He found it hard to connect with his new classmates and often felt lonely. Until one day, an incident changed everything. Bobby accidentally bumped into Clarisse Mei Tan of 9-India, causing a scene that would have lasting repercussions.

Chapter 4: Cracks Appear

After the incident, Bobby became more withdrawn and irritable. He started to turn all of his 9-Golf classmates into his enemies, and his behavior became increasingly erratic. He also made incidents like breaking someone's ukulele out of irritation, throwing food at his classmate's face and beating him up, and even forcing his classmate in an invisible chair position with all her books on her arms until dismissal.

Chapter 5: Further Triggers

Some of 9-Hotel's students notice Bobby walking by their classroom, and they came out of their classroom to confront him. They started by asking Bobby's section 9-Golf. He was bullied by 9-Hotel students, calling him names like "smartass", "nangongopya", and "always hangs out with girls". Bobby's anger was triggered, and he lashed out at the 9-Hotel students. At the end, the 9-Hotel students called him "Bading!" before returning to their classroom. Bobby was left feeling humiliated and more isolated than ever.

Chapter 6: "Why did you humiliate me?"

After Bobby was bullied by 9-Hotel students about his section, he lashed out at Janelle Marie Reyes, the class president of 9-Golf. He accused her of humiliating him, but she did not do anything to him. Bobby then beat Janelle up very hard.

Chapter 7: Karina vs. Clarisse

In 9-India, Karina Mae Arevalo confronts Clarisse Mei Tan about the incident with Bobby. Karina is furious at Clarisse for not letting Bobby enter the 9-India classroom, and she blames Clarisse for Bobby's current state. The confrontation escalates into a heated argument, with Karina accusing Clarisse of being selfish and uncaring. Clarisse tries to defend herself, but Karina is relentless in her accusations. The argument ends with Clarisse being replaced by Karina as the class president of 9-India, and Clarisse is left feeling hurt and betrayed.

Chapter 8: Lawrence

Lawrence Allan Solinap of 9-Golf is the only student who tries to befriend Bobby, but Bobby's anger and paranoia make it difficult for him to accept Lawrence's friendship. Despite Lawrence's efforts, Bobby accepts his friendship.

Chapter 9: Welcome to Legends of Arcania!

During a school e-sports event, "Legends of Arcania," 9-Golf places last. Bobby erupts, shouting at classmates and threatening anyone who "tests his patience." However, 9-India was the champion, while 9-Hotel and 10-Lima were the second and third place, respectively. Bobby's anger reaches a boiling point, and he lashes out at his classmates, blaming them for the loss and accusing them of not taking the competition seriously.

Chapter 10: Faculty Concerns

The faculty of Dalisay High School becomes increasingly concerned about Bobby's behavior and the potential danger he poses to his classmates. They discuss possible interventions and strategies to address the situation, but they also struggle with how to balance Bobby's needs with the safety of the other students.

Chapter 11: Hallucinations

Bobby begins experiencing hallucinations, seeing things that aren't there. His classmates (in 9-Golf) and teachers become more concerned about his mental state. The school's counselor is brought in to assess Bobby's condition and provide support. These include Simon washing his hands with blood, and Bobby (as a demon-like figure) jumpscaring Amelia while she is decorating the classroom exterior.

Chapter 12: Karina Reports Amelia to Bobby

While Amelia Nicole Santos and her classmate is heading to their 9-Golf classroom, Amelia casually mentioned the name of Bobby's katropa and bestfriend in 9-India, Jerome Barry Sison. Karina Mae Arevalo, who was overprotective of Bobby, heard this and told her that she will tell Bobby about it. A crying Amelia refused Karina to tell Bobby about it, but Karina told her that if not to Bobby, she will tell the principal about it. Even though Amelia begged Karina not to tell Bobby about it, Karina still told Bobby about it.

Chapter 13: The Coercive Apology

After Karina told Bobby about Amelia mentioning Jerome's name, Bobby was furious. He confronted Amelia, violently kicked her seat, making her fall to the ground, and dragged her to the 9-India classroom, where his katropa were. Bobby forced Amelia to apologize to his katropa, and he threatened her that if she doesn't apologize to them, he will kill her. Amelia was forced to apologize to them, and she was left feeling humiliated and traumatized. Additionally, Bobby told her that if she tells anyone about this, he will kill her. Amelia was left feeling scared and alone, and she struggled to cope with the trauma of the incident.

Chapter 14: Why, Amelia?

Following the incident where Bobby forced Amelia to apologize to his katropa, Amelia visited her boyfriend Anjhelo Mikael Del Rosario's classroom 10-Lima crying. Anjhelo was shocked to see Amelia crying, and he asked her what happened. Amelia told him about the incident with Bobby, however, Karina's close friend Zinnia Valencia, also from 9-India, noticed Amelia telling what Bobby did to her to Anjhelo, and she told Bobby about it.

Chapter 15: Suspension

Following the incident with Amelia the same day, Bobby was suspended from school for 3 days. During his suspension, he was left alone with his thoughts, and he struggled to cope with the consequences of his actions. The school community was left reeling from the incident, and there were discussions about how to prevent similar incidents from happening in the future. His parents, Rico and Teresa, are mad at him for his behavior at school, and even read the messages that were sent to him by Karina and Zinnia, which made them even more mad at him.

Chapter 16: It Doesn't End Here

After 3 days of suspension, Bobby returned to school. 9-Golf students were still scared of him, and they avoided him as much as possible. Bobby's behavior did not improve. Teachers are concerned about his mental state, and they are unsure of how to help him. He wrote an alarming death threat message in red permanent marker on the whiteboard of the classroom, which made the students and teachers even more scared of him. The school community is left wondering what will happen next, and they are all hoping for a resolution that will prevent any further harm.

Chapter 17: Lagot Ka, Amelia

On the same day, Bobby heard about the message sent by Zinnia Valencia reporting that Amelia told Anjhelo about the incident with him, and he was furious. He confronted Amelia, threatening her to "get ready the following day". That night, Bobby puts a knife in his bag, and he was planning to attack Amelia the following day. However, his mom Teresa found the knife in his bag, and she was shocked and scared. Bobby just explained that it's for their TLE class, but Teresa was still scared and worried about him.

Chapter 18: The Breaking Point

The following day, during recess time, Bobby approached Amelia in the 9-Golf classroom, and he was holding a knife. He threatened Amelia to run with him chasing her, and he implied that if he catches her, he will kill her. Amelia was terrified and ran as fast as she can, but Bobby was faster than her. He caught her, and he stabbed her 47 times, which made her die. She was left in a pool of her own blood, and the school community was left in shock and horror. Bobby then ran to the 9-India classroom crying because he missed his katropa, and he was crying that he wants to be with them again.

Chapter 19: Expelled

After the stabbing incident, Bobby was immediately expelled from school. The school community was left in shock and mourning, and there were discussions about how to prevent similar incidents from happening in the future. Bobby's parents, Rico and Teresa, were even more mad at him for his actions, and they were left wondering how to deal with the consequences of his actions. The school community was left wondering what will happen next, and they are all hoping for a resolution that will prevent any further harm. The same day, Bobby was arrested by the police, but since he was a minor, he was sent to a juvenile detention center. The school community was left wondering what will happen to Bobby, and they are all hoping for a resolution that will prevent any further harm.

Chapter 20: Farewell, Amelia

Amelia Nicole Santos' wake was held at her family's house, and it was attended by her family, friends, classmates, and teachers. The school community was left in shock and mourning, and there were discussions about how to prevent similar incidents from happening in the future. Amelia's family, including her parents, Regina and Arman, and her younger brother Ryder Miguel, as well as Anjhelo Mikael Del Rosario, her boyfriend, was also left devastated by her death. Days later, she was buried in a cemetery.

Chapter 21: Remembering Amelia

Amelia Nicole Santos' family and friends, including Anjhelo Mikael Del Rosario, her boyfriend, and her classmates and teachers, remember her as a kind and caring person who was always there for others. They share their memories of her and how she impacted their lives. The school community also comes together to honor her memory and to support each other in the aftermath of the tragedy. Her mother Regina even became upset while remembering Amelia, and she cried while remembering her daughter. Anjhelo also cried while remembering Amelia, and he said that he will never forget her. However, her relatives try to comfort her mother Regina, and they told her that Amelia is in a better place now. They also try to comfort each other, and they told each other that they will get through this together.

Chapter 22: The Trial

After 3 years of being in a juvenile detention center, Bobby, now 18 years old, was released and was put on trial for the murder of Amelia Nicole Santos. The trial was attended by the school community, including Amelia's family, Bobby's family, and their classmates and teachers. The trial was a long and emotional process, and it was left wondering what will happen to Bobby, and they are all hoping for a resolution that will prevent any further harm. After a lengthy legal process, Bobby was found guilty and sentenced to life in prison.

Amelia's family, along with her boyfriend Anjhelo Mikael Del Rosario and Janelle Marie Reyes, the once-class president of 9-Golf, visits Amelia's grave and pays their respects.
